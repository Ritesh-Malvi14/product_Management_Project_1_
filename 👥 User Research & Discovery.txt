## 👥 User Research & Discovery

### Research Methodology
- **Method:** 1:1 User Interviews (qualitative)
- **Sample Size:** 30+ undergraduate students across departments
- **Duration:** 2 weeks of discovery sprints

### Key User Personas

**Persona 1 — "The Overwhelmed Fresher"**
- Age: 18–20 | Semester: 1st–2nd
- Goal: Find notes and understand what topics to focus on before exams
- Frustration: Doesn't know the right people to ask; misses important updates

**Persona 2 — "The Collaborative Senior"**
- Age: 21–23 | Semester: 5th–6th
- Goal: Organize group study, share resources, track deadlines
- Frustration: Coordination happens across 4–5 apps with no single source of truth

### Research Insights
> *"I have notes from last year but no way to share them properly with juniors."*  
> *"Our WhatsApp group is so noisy — I miss actual important messages."*  
> *"I wish there was one app just for academics, not social stuff."*
