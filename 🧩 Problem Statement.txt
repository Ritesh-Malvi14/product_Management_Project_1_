## 🧩 Problem Statement

University students struggle with fragmented study resources — notes are scattered 
across WhatsApp groups, exam dates are missed, and there's no structured space for 
subject-specific peer collaboration.

After conducting **user interviews with 30+ students**, the following core pain points emerged:

| Pain Point | Frequency Mentioned |
|---|---|
| No centralized subject-wise discussion space | 87% |
| Missed exam dates / no shared academic calendar | 73% |
| Difficulty accessing peer notes before exams | 68% |
| Feeling academically isolated in large batches | 61% |
