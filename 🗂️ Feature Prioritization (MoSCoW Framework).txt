## 🗂️ Feature Prioritization — MoSCoW Framework

| Priority | Feature | Rationale |
|---|---|---|
| **Must Have** | Subject-specific chat rooms | Core pain point for 87% of users |
| **Must Have** | Shared notes repository | Directly reduces exam prep friction |
| **Must Have** | Exam & deadline calendar | Missed dates = highest student anxiety trigger |
| **Should Have** | Study group formation | Enables peer collaboration at scale |
| **Should Have** | Note rating & upvote system | Ensures quality content surfaces first |
| **Could Have** | AI-powered study reminders | Increases retention and engagement |
| **Won't Have (v1)** | Video calling / live sessions | Scope risk; deferred to v2 roadmap |
