## 💡 Key PM Learnings

- **Say No Strategically:** Deferring video calls to v2 kept the MVP focused and shippable within 3 months
- **Research before building:** 30+ interviews prevented building features students didn't actually need
- **Align roadmap to release cycles:** Breaking work into 3 phases reduced scope creep and kept the team focused
- **MoSCoW works:** Prioritization framework helped stakeholders align quickly without endless debates
